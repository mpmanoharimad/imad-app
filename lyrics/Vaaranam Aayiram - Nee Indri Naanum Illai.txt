Vaaranam Aayiram

Nee indri naanum illai
En kaathal poyyum illai
Vazhi engum unthan mugam thaan
Vali kooda inge sugam thaan

Thoduvaanam sivanthu pogum
Tholai thooram kurainthu pogum
Karaiginra nodigalil naan nerungi vanthene
Ini unnai piriya maataen
Thuli thooram nagara maataen
Mugam paarka thavikkiraen en iniya poongatre
Oh shanthi shanthi oh shanthi
En uyirai uyirai nee enthi
En sendrai sendrai enai neengi?
Naan vanthaen vanthaen unai thedi
Nee indri naanum illai
En kaathal poyyum illai
Unai kaanum neram varuma?
Iru kangal mootcham peruma?
Viralodu vizhiyum vaadum
Viraiginra kaalam nogum
Irunthaalum varugiraen un madiyil naan thoonga

Enai vanthu urasum kaatre
Avalodu kanavil netre
Kaikorthu nerunginaen kan adithu nee yenga

Oh shanthi shanthi oh shanthi
En uyirai uyirai nee enthi
En sendrai sendrai enai neengi
